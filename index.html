SMS Spirituality

The spirituality of the Servite Mission Sisters is rooted in the tradition of the Servite Order . It is a Marian path inspired by the Blessed Virgin Mary under the title of Our Lady of Sorrows.

Click for More
>

Our charism is consecrated presence within society through compassionate witness, accompaniment, and service. Our mission is rooted in our consecration and carried out through apostolic works that respond to the spiritual, social, and human needs of the communities we serve.

The secular institute is often misunderstood. It does not mean non-religious. It is a true form of consecrated life lived fully within society among the people we serve

Vocation

The Servite Family brings together different vocations, each living the same call to consecrated life in distinct but complementary ways, united in a common spirit, mission, and service to God and His people.

Each vocation offers a unique way of giving one’s life to God in service, according to the grace and calling given to each person.

✝️ Friars

Men living their vocation in community through priesthood, brotherhood, and pastoral ministry.

⛪ Monks

Men dedicated to a life of prayer, contemplation, and monastic community.

🙏 Contemplative Sisters

Women dedicated to silence, prayer, and contemplation in community.

🤝 Apostolic Sisters

Women serving through education, healthcare, and missionary outreach.

👥 Secular Institute Sisters

Women living their vocation within society through witness and service.

❤️ Lay Servite Members

Men and women living Servite spirituality in family and professional life.
