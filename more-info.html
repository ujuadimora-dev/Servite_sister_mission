Servite Mission Sisters

Living the Gospel in the heart of society

We are Servite Mission Sisters, consecrated women who have given their lives entirely to God through the evangelical counsels of poverty, chastity, and obedience. Rooted in prayer and committed to service, we live our vocation faithfully within the realities of everyday life.

Rooted in the spirituality of the Servite tradition, we live our vocation fully within society. We serve God in humanity and bring His presence into everyday life. Our calling is not separate from the world.

We live within it, allowing God to work through prayer, witness, and service. As part of the wider international Servite Secular Institute, an international pontifical institution, our expression in Africa responds to the spiritual and pastoral needs of the people.

We remain faithful to the Church and meaningful within the cultural context. We are committed to works of service that touch lives in both practical and spiritual ways.

We walk with individuals, support communities, and witness to the love of Christ in all circumstances.

Our Mission

Our charism is consecrated presence within society through compassionate witness, accompaniment, and service. Our mission is rooted in our consecration and carried out through apostolic works that respond to the spiritual, social, and human needs of the communities we serve. The secular institute is often misunderstood. It does not mean non-religious. It is a true form of consecrated life lived fully within society among the people we serve. Servite Mission Sisters engage in diverse ministries including health care, education, business, civil service, and community engagement, allowing their service to adapt according to emerging needs and circumstances.

Healthcare

Serving through healthcare and compassionate care for people in need.

Education

Supporting learning, knowledge, and the development of lives.

Community Life

Walking with communities through faith, support, and service.

Apostolate

Serving through professional work, mission, and dedication.

Through these ministries we seek to bring the presence of Christ into the realities of life and to serve with faith, integrity, and dedication.

A Call to Give Your Life to God-Vocation

The Servite Family brings together different vocations, each living the same call to consecrated life in distinct but complementary ways, united in a common spirit, mission, and service to God and His people. Each vocation offers a unique way of giving one’s life to God in service, according to the grace and calling given to each person.

Have you ever felt a deeper desire to belong completely to God? A quiet call to serve Him, not away from the world, but within it?

The Servite Mission Sisters are consecrated women who have responded to this call. They offer their lives entirely to God through the evangelical counsels of poverty, chastity, and obedience, while living and serving within society.

Rooted in the spirituality of the Servite tradition, this vocation is lived in the reality of everyday life through prayer, service, and witness.

As part of the wider international Servite Secular Institute, an international pontifical institution, this way of life is both deeply spiritual and fully engaged with the needs of the world today.

Formation Journey

Formation in the Institute is a gradual and structured process, usually taking place4 over a period of four years. It unfolds progressively, allowing each person to grow spiritually, mature personally, and deepen her understanding of the vocation and mission of the Institute.

01

Candidacy

1–2 Years

A time of introduction, discernment, and initial formation. During this stage, the candidate begins to understand the nature of the vocation and to grow in prayer and personal commitment.

02

Formation Period

2–3 Years

A time of deeper growth, commitment, and integration of the vocation into daily life. This stage helps the candidate to live the vocation more consciously and consistently within her personal and professional environment.

03

Profession

Final Commitment

A formal commitment to God through the evangelical counsels of chastity, poverty, and obedience, marking full incorporation into the Institute.

Progress Through Formation: Each person’s journey is unique. Progress between stages depends on readiness, commitment, and ongoing discernment. The process is accompanied with guidance, prayer, and careful evaluation at each stage.
A Journey of Growth : Formation is not rushed. It is a careful and prayerful journey that allows each person to respond freely and faithfully to God’s call, growing in stability, responsibility, and dedication.

WHO WE ARE

Our Identity - Servite Mission Sisters in the African Region are part of the International Servite Secular Institute of the Servants of Mary, a consecrated institute of pontifical right approved by the Church.

Our Way of Life - Our way of life is rooted in our consecration to God and expressed through service. We are women of prayer, living our vocation with commitment and responsibility within society.

Our life is lived through: prayer, service, professional and social engagement, faithfulness in daily responsibilities.

Some live individually, while others live in small communities, according to the life and structure of the Institute.

Our Spirituality - The spirituality of the Servite Mission Sisters is rooted in the tradition of the Servite Order. It is a Marian path inspired by the Blessed Virgin Mary under the title of Our Lady of Sorrows. We are called to remain steadfast in faith, to stand firm in times of difficulty, and to accompany others with compassion and quiet strength.

Prayer for Our Mission

Prayer of Consecration for the African Mission and Beyond

O Lord our God, unless You build this work, we labour in vain, and unless You watch over it, all our efforts are empty. We place before You the establishment, the growth, and the mission of the Servite Mission Sisters in every place where You choose to plant and expand it.

We commit this mission and all its work to You. Guide every step, direct every decision, and align everything with Your will and Your time. Go before this mission, O God of our salvation. Grant what is needed according to Your purpose and bring to fulfilment what You have begun.

Build what should be built. Protect what should be protected. Establish what must remain. Open the doors that come from You and close those that do not. Let this mission grow, serve, and remain for Your glory alone.
