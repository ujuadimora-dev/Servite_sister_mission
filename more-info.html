Servite Mission Sisters

Living the Gospel in the heart of society

We are Servite Mission Sisters, consecrated women who have given their lives entirely to God through the evangelical counsels of poverty, chastity, and obedience.

Rooted in the spirituality of the Servite tradition, we live our vocation fully within society. We serve God in humanity and bring His presence into everyday life. Our calling is not separate from the world.

We live within it, allowing God to work through prayer, witness, and service. As part of the wider international Servite Secular Institute, an international pontifical institution, our expression in Africa responds to the spiritual and pastoral needs of the people.

We remain faithful to the Church and meaningful within the cultural context. We are committed to works of service that touch lives in both practical and spiritual ways.

We walk with individuals, support communities, and witness to the love of Christ in all circumstances.

Our Mission

Our mission is rooted in our consecration to God and expressed through service in the Church and in society.

Healthcare

Serving through healthcare and compassionate care for people in need.

Education

Supporting learning, knowledge, and the development of lives.

Community Life

Walking with communities through faith, support, and service.

Apostolate

Serving through professional work, mission, and dedication.

Through these ministries we seek to bring the presence of Christ into the realities of life and to serve with faith, integrity, and dedication.

A Call to Give Your Life to God

Have you ever felt a deeper desire to belong completely to God? A quiet call to serve Him, not away from the world, but within it?

The Servite Mission Sisters are consecrated women who have responded to this call. They offer their lives entirely to God through the evangelical counsels of poverty, chastity, and obedience, while living and serving within society.

Rooted in the spirituality of the Servite tradition, this vocation is lived in the reality of everyday life through prayer, service, and witness.

As part of the wider international Servite Secular Institute, an international pontifical institution, this way of life is both deeply spiritual and fully engaged with the needs of the world today.

Our Way of Life

Our life is centred on prayer, service, and faithful witness.

We serve individuals and communities in their daily realities. We live the Gospel in simple and practical ways. In all we do, we seek to make the love of Christ present.

We are committed to works of service that touch lives both spiritually and practically, walking with people, supporting them, and being a sign of God’s presence in their lives.

Prayer for Our Mission

Prayer of Consecration for the African Mission and Beyond

O Lord our God, unless You build this work, we labour in vain, and unless You watch over it, all our efforts are empty. We place before You the establishment, the growth, and the mission of the Servite Mission Sisters in every place where You choose to plant and expand it.

We commit this mission and all its work to You. Guide every step, direct every decision, and align everything with Your will and Your time. Go before this mission, O God of our salvation. Grant what is needed according to Your purpose and bring to fulfilment what You have begun.

Build what should be built. Protect what should be protected. Establish what must remain. Open the doors that come from You and close those that do not. Let this mission grow, serve, and remain for Your glory alone.
